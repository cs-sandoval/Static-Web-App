Available Pets for Adoption

Meet Athena, a 7-year-old German-Shepherd/Husky Mix! She is house trained, having perfect recall to her name, and likes to greet people by waving and speaking!

Like Luna and Kila, she is bilingual in English and Spanish!

She is currently up for adoption because she was framed for a steak from her owners plate (she swears it was the husky).

Meet Luna, a 7 year old Siberian Husky! She is house trained (when she wants to be) and knows tons of tricks (when she wants to show them off) such as sit, touch, high five, and sing!

Like Athena and Kila, she is bilingual, understanding both English and Spanish!

She is currently up for adoption because she was caught steaking a potato from her owners plate (She denies any allegations that she stole the steak as well).

Meet Kila, a 6 year old German-Shepherd dog! She is a work dog who has settled down into a comfortable lazy life. She loves to retrieve things, and will go searching for items based on scents!

Like Athena and Luna, she is bilingual, understanding both English and Spanish!

She is currently up for adoption because she had dropped a tennis ball on her owners face while they were sleeping one too many times.

Meet Ash, a 5 year old white cat who looks like he has been drawn to life by cartoon artists. He is the goodest of boys, and loves to cuddle on laps!

Ash was born deaf and has heterochromia, one eye being blue and the other being green!

He is currently up for adoption because he has turned off his owners computer far too many times.
