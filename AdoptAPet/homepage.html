About Us

We are a non-profit helping rehome pets who are in need of new homes! All of our current pets available for adoption come well trained and super friendly!

Pets are amazing companions, they are amazing examples of unconditional love - some of them have just had bad luck in life and find themselves in need of a new home, consider being that for them! We make it easy to find your next friend, locally! As of right now, we are limited to the Piedmont area of North Carolina, but with plans to expand our area soon!

All applications are processed and reviewed within 5-7 business days.

Adopt, Don't Shop!

Choosing adoption contributes to the welfare of animals and gives them a second chance at life!

Why spend $1,200 on an untrained puppy when you can get a free fully trained dog!

Each application and adoption is free! Simply just fill out our easy application forms to proceed to the next steps of adoption!
